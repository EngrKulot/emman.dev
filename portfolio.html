About Me

Hello, I'm Emman

I’m a Web Developer and GoHighLevel (GHL) Specialist dedicated to building modern, user-friendly, and high-performing digital solutions.

On the web development side, I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, creating sleek, responsive websites that deliver exceptional user experiences. My goal is to bridge aesthetics with functionality, ensuring every project not only looks professional but also works seamlessly across devices.

On the automation side, I leverage GoHighLevel (GHL) to design powerful workflows, funnels, and integrations that streamline processes for lead generation, customer engagement, and business growth. I also work with Make.com and ManyChat to build smart automations that help businesses operate more efficiently.

As a 25-year-old Filipino Web Developer and Automation Specialist, I’m passionate about combining front-end development and business automation to create impactful digital experiences. I’m always eager to take on new challenges, collaborate on innovative projects, and help businesses scale through technology.
